Serj Tankian

"Serj Tankian" was a project I made in my Electronic Imaging class, where we were supposed to create an image of a person we were inspired by, someone we like, using only speeches or quotes they said, in my case I did Serk Tankian using song lyrics of his band "System of a Down", and for the background I used the lyric of one of his solo songs "Baby"

Project Details

  • Created using Photoshop.
  • Took 5-6 hours to complete.
  • Black and White, but background has the colors of the Armenian flag.
  • Subject: Serj Tankian, singer.
